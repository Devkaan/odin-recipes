Odin Recipe

  1. Prep Time: 30 mins
  2. Cook Time: 1 hrs 20 mins
  3. Total Time: 1 hrs 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoon vegetabel oil
  • 4 small potatoes & 2 tomatoes
  • 2 large onions & 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger & salt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder & black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon yougurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 piece cinnamon stick
  • 3 pounds boneless,skinless chicken pieces
  • 1 pound basmati rice

Directions

Step 1


Heat oil in a large skillet.Fry potatoes until lightly brown Remove to a paper towel to drain and set aside.

Step 2


Add onions, garlic, and fresh ginger to the oil; cook until golden. Add the rest of the Ingredients and stir constantly
for 5 minutes. Add little water, cover and cook over low heat.

Step 3


Add chicken and stir well to coat. Cover and cook over very low heat until chicken is tender.

Step 4


Meanwhile wash rice well and drain in colander for at least 30 minutes.

Step 5


Add water to the chicken mixture and heat chicken stock until hot, pour over rice and stir well. Bring to a boil reduce heat
to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and steam for 20 minutes without lifting the lid. Spoon biryani onto a warm serving dish.
